1. Overview

Snapomat is a personal budget tracking app that helps you capture and understand your expenses. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

2. Data Controller

3. What Data We Process

Snapomat processes only data you enter yourself or capture by photo: expense amounts, merchant names, dates, categories, and your monthly budget. All this data is stored locally on your device. We have no access to your financial data.

4. AI Receipt Analysis

When you photograph a receipt, the image is transmitted to a secure server (Cloudflare Workers) and analyzed by Claude, an AI model developed by Anthropic. The image is used solely for text recognition and is not stored permanently on any server. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. Snapomat contains no advertising, no tracking, and no behavioral analytics.

6. Local Storage

All your expense data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. No data is transmitted to any server except receipt images for AI analysis (see Section 4).

7. Backup & Restore

The Backup & Restore feature creates a local JSON file on your device. You decide where to store or share this file. We have no access to your backup files.

8. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and credit packs are processed exclusively through Apple App Store or Google Play. Payment information is handled solely by Apple or Google. We do not process or store any payment data.

9. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. You can delete all your data at any time in Settings → Delete All Data.

10. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times. For any questions, contact us at tommi1008@gmail.com.

11. Children's Privacy

Snapomat is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
